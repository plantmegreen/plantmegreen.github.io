How to Successfully Plant Your Johnny Appleseed Authentic Apple Tree: Step-by-Step Guide

Introduction#

Are you a fan of apples and interested in growing your own apple trees? Look no further than the Johnny Appleseed Authentic Apple Tree! In this guide, we will walk you through the process of planting both bare root and container trees, ensuring that your tree has the best chance of thriving. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced gardener, follow these steps to successfully plant your Johnny Appleseed Authentic Apple Tree and enjoy the fruits of your labor.

Unboxing and Preparing the Tree#

First, let’s start with unboxing the Johnny Appleseed Authentic Apple Tree. The tree will come in a large cardboard box that is securely stapled shut. Carefully pry open the box, exerting some strength if necessary. Inside the box, you will find either a bare root tree or a container tree.

  • Bare root trees: If you receive a bare root tree, the roots will be neatly wrapped in a plastic bag filled with damp newspaper.
  • Container trees: For container trees, the roots will be contained in a plastic bag filled with soil.

Planting Bare Root Trees#

To ensure that your bare root tree thrives, you need to properly prepare it for planting. Follow these steps:

  1. Soak the roots: Before planting, soak the roots in water for one to two hours. This helps rehydrate the roots and prepare them for planting. However, it is important not to soak the roots for more than 24 hours, as this can cause damage.

  2. Dig the hole: Find a suitable spot in your garden and dig a hole large enough to accommodate the tree’s current root system, with some extra room for growth.

  3. Spread the roots: Once the hole is prepared, carefully spread the roots to encourage outward growth. This will help the tree establish a strong foundation.

  4. Plant the tree: Place the bare root tree in the hole, ensuring that it remains vertical and straight. If necessary, use stakes to provide support.

  5. Soil placement: The ideal position of the soil line should be visible on the trunk of the tree. Refill the hole with native soil, ensuring that the roots are well-covered. You may also add soil amendments if desired.

  6. Water thoroughly: After planting, thoroughly water your newly planted bare root tree. This will help settle the soil and provide essential moisture for the tree’s initial growth.

Planting Container Trees#

If you receive a container tree, follow these steps to ensure successful planting:

  1. Remove the tree: After opening the box, carefully remove the tree, ensuring that you remove any cardboard or packaging material around the pot.

  2. Dig the hole: Find a suitable location in your garden and dig a generous hole to accommodate the tree’s current roots and allow for future growth.

  3. Score the roots: Before planting, gently score the roots of the tree with a knife or pruners. This will help promote root growth and establish a stronger connection with the soil.

  4. Plant the tree: Center the root ball of the tree in the prepared hole, making sure that the soil level of the tree is even with the ground.

  5. Soil placement: Backfill the hole over the roots while keeping the trunk of the tree vertical and straight. This will provide stability and support for the tree.

  6. Water thoroughly: Lastly, give your newly planted container tree a thorough watering. This will help eliminate any air pockets and ensure that the tree’s roots receive adequate moisture.

Conclusion#

Congratulations! You have successfully learned how to plant your Johnny Appleseed Authentic Apple Tree. Whether you received a bare root or container tree, following these step-by-step instructions will give your tree the best chance of thriving. Remember to choose a suitable location for your tree, provide the necessary support if needed, and water it thoroughly after planting. With proper care and attention, you will soon be enjoying delicious and authentic apples from your very own Johnny Appleseed trees. Happy planting!
